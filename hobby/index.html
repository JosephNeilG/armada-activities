Benefits of Running

  • Improves heart health
  • Strengthens muscles and bones
  • Enhances mental well-being and reduces stress

Getting Started with Running

  1. Invest in a good pair of running shoes
  2. Start with short, easy runs and gradually increase distance
  3. Warm up before and cool down after each run
